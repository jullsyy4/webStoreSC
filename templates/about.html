About store

Online Spanish classes have become increasingly important for individuals who have left their homes and are now residing in Spain. The courses are available for anyone and include general group and individual Spanish lessons for beginners (A1) and A2-C1 levels, exam preparation, conversational Spanish, and distance courses on an interactive platform. In addition, there are intensive online Spanish courses available in Spain for refugees from Ukraine, taught by Ukrainian instructors. Our goal is to provide quality education and support to our students.
